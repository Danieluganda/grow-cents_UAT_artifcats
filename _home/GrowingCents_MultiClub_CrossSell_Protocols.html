Growing Cents: Multi-Club & Cross-Selling Protocols v1.0



Guidelines for institutional isolation, regional governance, and strategic monetization prompts.

1. Multi-Club Governance



1.1 Subscription Isolation


  • Each Club (Institutional Node) requires a unique Subscription ID.
  • Data from Club A is strictly firewalled from Club B unless viewed by an L1 (Super Admin) or L3 (Regional Manager).


1.2 Selection Logic


  • Post-authentication, users with multiple club associations are presented with the Elite Entity Selector.
  • Selecting a node initializes the specific dashboard environment for that institution.


2. Cross-Selling & Monetization



2.1 Upsell Triggers


  • Trigger A: "Low Maturity Score" (< 30%) -> Prompt: "Unlock Expert Consultation Package".
  • Trigger B: "Audit Limit Reached" -> Prompt: "Upgrade to Institutional Unlimited".


2.2 Conditions for Report Unlocking


  • Successful `charge.success` webhook pulse.
  • Assessment status marked as `verified` by High Command.
