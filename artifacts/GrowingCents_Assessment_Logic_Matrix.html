2. Institutional Grouping Protocol


The 9 diagnostic areas are clustered into 3 strategic pillars for high-level reporting.

Pillar A: Strategic Governance & Records


Areas: 1 (Structure), 3 (Constitution), 6 (Governance)
  • Logic Node: Focuses on legal stability and accountability structures.
  • Weighting: 35% of Overall Maturity.

    • Pillar B: Financial Performance & Internal Controls

    Areas: 2 (Strategy), 4 (Banking), 7 (Stake)
  • Logic Node: Focuses on liquidity, audit trails, and capital resilience.
  • Weighting: 40% of Overall Maturity.

    • Pillar C: Member Experience & Sustainability

    Areas: 5 (Meetings), 8 (Welfare), 9 (Engagement)
  • Logic Node: Focuses on retention, pulse, and long-term socio-economic impact.
  • Weighting: 25% of Overall Maturity.

    • 3. Operational Logic Rules

    3.1 The 7-Day Grace Period

    Upon completion of a level 3-5 assessment, a Strict 168-hour (7 day) lock is applied.
  • Node Action: Edits are permitted within this window only.
  • Audit Trigger: Any post-submission change triggers a "Refactored Score" flag in the dashboard.

    • 3.2 Automated Recommendation Engine

    Logic for generating L1-L6 action plans:
  • Priority HIGH: Applied to any Section scoring < 50%.
  • Priority MEDIUM: Applied to any Section scoring 51-75%.
  • Protocol: "High Priority" items must be addressed before the next 2-quarter audit cycle.


3.3 Maturity Calculation (Geometric Mean)


To prevent "Score Puffing" (offsetting a critical failure with a high score elsewhere), the system prioritizes consistent performance across all nodes. A zero-score in a critical banking node will significantly drag down the total maturity regardless of other high scores.
