Operational Known Issues

This document lists all known bugs, gaps, and limitations in the current UAT build of the Growing Cents platform. These are confirmed against the actual codebase. All items are tracked in the implementation plan with assigned priorities and fix timelines.

⚠️ Critical: Items marked P0 will cause immediate test failures on Day 1 of UAT execution. These must be resolved before Moses begins testing.

4. Known Limitations (By Design)

  • Payment gateway (Pesapal) is not integrated — deferred pending merchant account. All reports accessible without subscription during UAT.
  • Spatie roles are platform-wide, not club-specific. Club-level permission enforcement relies on club_id scoping in controllers rather than per-club permission checks. See RBAC Model v2.0 Gap #6.
  • SMS OTP delivery is not implemented — OTP is email-only during UAT. SRS Section 4 notes SMS as a future integration.
  • Auto-suggest for existing clubs during registration is not yet implemented (A1.2).
  • Join-request workflow (user-initiated) is not yet implemented — current flow is admin-push invite only (A2.1).
